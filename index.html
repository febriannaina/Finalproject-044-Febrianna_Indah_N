About Me

An agriculture graduate who is passionate about digital marketing, especially social media and search engine optimization (SEO). My interest in digital marketing started in my third year of college when I became a Head of Media of my organization. Then, I chose to take a part time job as a Social Media Specialist to dive into this field.
